About Fortis India Law

A premier boutique law firm based in Mumbai.

Fortis India Law is a boutique law firm founded in 2013 by Rubina Khan. With an office in Nariman Point, Mumbai, the firm has grown to include a dedicated team of 20 aspiring lawyers. Our journey is defined by our relentless pursuit of justice for our clients, whether they are individuals fighting for their rights or corporations navigating complex regulatory landscapes.

We cater to a diverse clientele including leading Indian and multinational companies, public and private sector banks, financial institutions, and real estate developers. Our expertise spans corporate advisory, banking and finance, real estate, and dispute resolution.
